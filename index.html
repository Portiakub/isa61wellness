Psychosocial Support Services

Portia Kubheka is a qualified Social Worker and Therapist with a Bachelor of Social Work degree from UNISA and a Certificate in Employee Wellness from the University of Pretoria.

She attends training regularly to improve her knowledge in supporting employees experiencing stress, anxiety, depression and trauma.

Portia has over 10 years of experience working with individuals from diverse cultures and previously worked for the Open Door Crisis Care Centre for seven years.

Areas of Expertise

  • Trauma Debriefing – Loss, grief, divorce, hijacking
  • Stress, anger issues, depression and anxiety
  • Psychological, emotional and sexual assault support
  • Substance and alcohol abuse counselling
  • Family conflict and relationship counselling
  • Pre-marital counselling
  • Marriage counselling
  • Children behavioural issues and bullying
  • Gender based violence support
  • Parenting and co-parenting guidance
