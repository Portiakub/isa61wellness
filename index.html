Psychosocial Support Services

Portia Kubheka is a qualified Social Worker and Therapist with a Bachelor of Social Work degree from UNISA and a Certificate in Employee Wellness from the University of Pretoria.

She has over 10 years of professional experience supporting individuals experiencing stress, trauma, anxiety and depression.

Portia previously worked at the Open Door Crisis Care Centre for seven years providing counselling and psychosocial support.

  • Stress and Anxiety Counselling
  • Substance Abuse Support
  • Marriage and Relationship Counselling
  • Pre-marital Counselling
  • Gender Based Violence Support
  • Parenting Guidance
