Psychosocial Support Services

Portia Kubheka is a qualified Social Worker and Therapist. She holds a Bachelor of Social Work degree from UNISA, majoring in Psychology, and a Certificate in Employee Wellness from the University of Pretoria.

She attends training every two months to deepen her knowledge and improve her ability to support employees experiencing stress, anxiety, and depression.

Portia has over 10 years of experience serving individuals from diverse cultures. She worked for the Open Door Crisis Care Centre for seven years where she developed strong expertise in psychosocial support.
