Psychosocial Support Services

Portia Kubheka is a qualified Social Worker and Therapist. She holds a Bachelor of Social Work degree from UNISA, majoring in Psychology, and a Certificate in Employee Wellness from the University of Pretoria.

With over 10 years of experience working with diverse cultures, Portia previously served at the Open Door Crisis Care Centre for seven years where she gained deep expertise in psychosocial support and trauma intervention.

In August 2023 Portia launched her private practice, ISA61 Life Wellness, operating from Union Main Wellness in Pinetown.

Areas of Support

  • Trauma Debriefing – Loss, Grief, Bereavement, Divorce, Hijacking
  • Stress, Depression, Anxiety and Anger Management
  • Psychological, Emotional and Sexual Assault Support
  • Substance and Alcohol Abuse
  • Family Conflict and Relationship Issues
  • Pre-Marital Counselling
  • Marriage Counselling
  • Children Behavioural Issues and Bullying
  • Gender Based Violence Support
  • Parenting and Co-Parenting Skills

Corporate Wellness Services

Portia works with organisations to provide Employee Wellness interventions including One-on-One Counselling, Group Trauma Debriefing and Awareness Programs.
